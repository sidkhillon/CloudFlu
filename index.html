Cloud Flu

CloudFlu is a revolutionary app that can notify you of the most recent disease/sickness/flu outbreak near you, allowing you to be more safe and aware of diseases near you. Once it is up and running, CloudFlu can start tracking the most recent and nearest outbreaks using various sources of media to inform you of possible outbreaks.
